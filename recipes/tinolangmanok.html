Tinolang Manok/Chicken Tinola

Description

A very healthy chicken and spinach ginger-based soup. My favorite dish of all time, my grandmother would cook this for me often. Cooking and eating it reminds me of her.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 36 oz. rice washing, or water
  • 1/2 pc. green papaya, cut into wedges
  • 1 cup hot pepper leaves or malunggay leaves or spinach
  • 1 tbsp. garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 thumb ginger, cut into strips
  • 2 tbsp. fish sauce
  • 1 pc. Knorr chicken cube
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper or whole black peppercorns

Cooking Steps

  1. Saut√© the garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Put in the chicken and cook until color turns light brown.
  3. Add the fish sauce and Knorr chicken cube. Stir. Pour rice washing or water into the cooking pot. Let boil. Cover the pot and simmer for 45 minutes. Note: add more water if needed.
  4. Add green papaya and whole peppercorns (if used). Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the hot pepper leaves/malunggay leaves or spinach. Stir and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Season with ground black pepper. Note you can also add more fish sauce or salt to taste.
  7. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve hot with a cup of warm steamed white rice. Share and enjoy!
